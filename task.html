About us

Located in one of the liveliest and most authentic districts of Paris, Le Regent Montmartre is located a few steps from the Gare du Nord. It is surrounded by hundreds of bars, pubs and cafés, as well as famous landmarks such as the Sacré Cœur, the Moulin Rouge and Pigalle.

Take the Orlyval shuttle to the RER B – Antony station. Take RER B direction Charles-de-Gaulle airport. Get off at the Gare du Nord station. Exit the station Rue de Dunkerque and walk up the street to Boulevard Rochechouart.
